SIMPLE STUDENT CARBONARA

This recipe presents a basic version of carbonara pasta, designed for students. The content focuses on clear steps, organization, and readability while following the same ROG-inspired design system used across the portfolio.

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g spaghetti pasta
  • 1 cup all-purpose cream
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Grated cheese (optional)

PROCEDURE

  1. Boil water in a pot, add salt, and cook the spaghetti until tender.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the garlic until lightly golden.
  3. Add the all-purpose cream and simmer over low heat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then stir gently.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and mix well.
  6. Top with grated cheese before serving.

This dish demonstrates a simple cooking process presented in a clean and structured layout suitable for academic requirements.
